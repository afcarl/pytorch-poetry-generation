


 Over the streets are frivolous with snow. 
 The crows have borne their eyebrows to the air's bells 
 And strut up their strings so 
 About him the uncertain Yankee whistles 
 Dropt in his throat. He hath them drawn like a droning stream, 
 Stops and separated by wine on a four-leaf head: 
 Sea of touted which from the very crest 
 And processions gather in the basement domes 
 What else good, surely to reach this sigh;
