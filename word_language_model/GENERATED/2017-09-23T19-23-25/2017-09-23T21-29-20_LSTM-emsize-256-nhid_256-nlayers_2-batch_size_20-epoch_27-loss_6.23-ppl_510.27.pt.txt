
The Earth'S Wrath 

 As a roof lamp once crossed 
 Again to land they lost up 
 And over the emptiness. 
 
 And they whose powers were neither love, 
 Whereof aught mad the brazen side grow: 
 Or but Lethe the horizon's air. 
 
 Alone, Hemming some pile each other white 
 Off within the silence, and Carthage 
 A tender spirit, and the honey door, 
 Beneath the mountain tides I go,
