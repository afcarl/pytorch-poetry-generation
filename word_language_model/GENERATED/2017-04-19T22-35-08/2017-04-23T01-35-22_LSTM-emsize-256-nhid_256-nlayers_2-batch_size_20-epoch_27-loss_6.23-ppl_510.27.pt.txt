
Take Away The Roar 

 Up to the vast sea, 
 Herself to pluck the nations: coals, 
 And in the vault, they cast them down again. 
 
 When the shrill Poet moves as red as air, 
 No one heard a steed to lay: 
 But it is that that feeling never said.
