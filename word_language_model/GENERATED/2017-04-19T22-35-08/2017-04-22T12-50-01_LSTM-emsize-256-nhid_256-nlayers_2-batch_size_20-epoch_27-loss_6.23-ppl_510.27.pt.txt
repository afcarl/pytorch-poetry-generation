
At The Stone Window Of Pairs 

 Drink away from a hemlock sneer, 
 Even towns too can bless preening. 
 
 Unto the night there's a house 
 Of salt, Mozart and a goat, 
 And the great surf --Tommy 
 Competitive door;-- outgrabe. 
 
 In the sea of sleep, 
 This breed of night.
